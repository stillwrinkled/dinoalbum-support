About the App

DinoAlbum is an offline educational app featuring high-quality, photo-like images of dinosaurs and prehistoric creatures. Ideal for kids on long flights, road trips, or quiet time at home. No ads, no sign-in, and no personal data required.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the app free?
Yes, the starter pack is free. Additional packs can be unlocked via one-time purchases.

Q: Does the app work offline?
Yes, the app is fully functional without an internet connection once installed.
